⚙️ How Does It Work?

Step-by-step working process of the robot

📡

Detect

The ultrasonic sensor emits sound waves and measures the distance of obstacles ahead (range: 2cm–400cm).

🧠

Process

The Arduino UNO reads the sensor data and decides whether to move forward, stop, or turn in a direction.

🔄

Scan

The servo motor rotates the sensor left and right to scan a wider area and find the best available path.

🚗

Move

The L293D motor driver receives signals from Arduino and drives all 4 DC motors in the correct direction.
